A day at Little Sprouts

Predictable rhythm, endless discovery

Young children thrive on routine. Here's how a typical day flows — balancing active play, quiet time, learning, and rest.

  1. 7:00

    Arrival & free play

    Warm greetings and gentle activities to start the day.

  2. 8:30

    Breakfast

    A healthy, family-style morning meal together.

  3. 9:15

    Circle & learning

    Songs, stories, and our theme of the week.

  4. 11:00

    Outdoor play

    Running, climbing, and fresh-air adventures.

  5. 12:00

    Lunch & quiet time

    A nourishing meal followed by rest and naps.

  6. 3:00

    Snack & centers

    Art, blocks, sensory, and dramatic play.

  7. 5:00

    Wind-down & pickup

    Reflecting on the day and happy goodbyes.
